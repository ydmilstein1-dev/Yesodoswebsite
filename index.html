Our Mission

Premier Jewish and secular education with each child in mind.

Formerly Maalos, Yesodos has been redesigned for students who need more than a regular classroom can offer. Our program helps girls succeed scholastically, socially, and emotionally through individualized goals, devoted teachers, and practical tools for life.

  • Small, thoughtful supports that protect each student's confidence.
  • Clear academic goals paired with emotional safety and belonging.
  • A school environment where parents can feel heard and guided.
