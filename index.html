Our Mission

Premier Jewish and secular special education for girls who learn differently.

Yesodos supports girls whose learning needs call for more than ordinary classrooms can offer. In the positive, nurturing environment of Bais Rochel, each student is guided by individualized academic and behavioral plans that grow and change with her progress.

  • Students who need more targeted instruction and support than a traditional classroom can provide.
  • Students who may continue to struggle, or seem to be failing, even with resource room support.
  • Students who benefit from steady guidance as they grow scholastically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Team

Parents entrust us with this important responsibility. Our caring and devoted staff and teachers empower each student to grow in her own unique way, helping her reach her potential with tools and skills for all aspects of life.

  • Mrs. Cohen, BCBA/LBA, creates behavior plans and conducts social skills groups for our students.
  • Mrs. Stefansky, principal, is the spearhead of Yesodos.
  • Mrs. Friedman, program coordinator, oversees the social skills program, navigates funding, and maximizes benefits for our students.
  • Mrs. Schlesinger, intake advisor, is the trailblazer of our unique program and conducts initial evaluations, providing her expert recommendations.
  • Mrs. Milstein, program director, guides the program and pulls it all together.
