About Yesodos

Built for students who need a more personal path.

Yesodos is a Special Education program offering premier Jewish and secular education in a positive, nurturing Bais Rochel environment.

Mission

Helping each student succeed scholastically, socially, and emotionally.

Formerly Maalos, Yesodos has been redesigned to bring out the best in students who learn differently. The program serves girls who struggle because they need more than the regular classroom has to offer, including students who may still be falling behind even with resource room support.

  • Individualized academic and behavioral plans for each student.
  • Goals that change according to progress.
  • Caring teachers with experience, education, and practical skill.
  • Integration opportunities within the warm environment of Bais Rochel.

Approach

Different learners deserve strong foundations.

"Empowering each student to grow in her own unique way, our graduates are equipped with tools and skills for all aspects of life."

Yesodos mission

"Your daughter will be empowered to grow, her way, through support that addresses academic and social needs."
